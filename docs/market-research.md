# Market Research Report: Project TillLess

**Session Topic:** Market research for South African shopping optimization system
**Date:** 2025-09-25
**Facilitator:** Mary (Business Analyst)

## Research Objectives & Methodology

### Research Objectives

The primary objectives of this market research are to:

- **Validate market opportunity:** Assess the size and growth potential of the price comparison/shopping optimization market in South Africa
- **Understand target customers:** Profile South African households who would benefit from shopping optimization tools
- **Analyze competitive landscape:** Map existing solutions (global and local) and identify differentiation opportunities
- **Inform product strategy:** Guide feature prioritization, pricing approach, and go-to-market strategy for TillLess
- **Support investment decisions:** Provide data to justify development time and resources

**Success Criteria:**
- Clear understanding of addressable market size in South Africa
- Validated customer segments and their willingness to pay
- Competitive positioning strategy with clear differentiation
- Actionable recommendations for MVP development and launch approach

### Research Methodology

**Data Sources Used:**
- Statistics South Africa demographic and economic data
- Industry reports on South African retail and e-commerce
- International market analysis from analogous markets (US, UK, India)
- Consumer behavior studies and digital adoption surveys

**Analysis Frameworks Applied:**
- TAM/SAM/SOM market sizing with sensitivity analysis
- Customer segment profiling with Jobs-to-be-Done methodology
- Competitive landscape mapping and Porter's Five Forces
- Strategic scenario planning (pessimistic/base/optimistic cases)

**Data Collection Timeframe:** September 2025
**Key Limitations:** Limited primary research data; reliance on secondary sources and analogous market analysis

## Market Overview

### Market Definition

**Product/Service Category:** Shopping optimization and price comparison platform for household essentials

**Geographic Scope:** South Africa, with initial focus on major metropolitan areas (Johannesburg, Cape Town, Durban, Pretoria)

**Customer Segments:** Tech-comfortable households with regular grocery shopping needs and price optimization motivation

**Value Chain Position:** Consumer-facing optimization tool positioned between retailers and end customers, with potential for data monetization

### Market Size & Growth

#### Market Sizing Analysis with Sensitivity Scenarios

**Base Case Market Sizing (TAM):**

**South African Household Market:**
- Total households: ~17.2 million (Stats SA 2022)
- Internet-connected households: ~13.8 million (80%)
- Smartphone penetration: ~12.1 million (70% of households)
- Regular grocery shoppers: ~16.5 million (96% of households)

**Target Market Assumptions:**
- Households doing monthly grocery shops: ~12.9 million (75%)
- Households spending >R2,000/month on groceries: ~8.6 million (50%)
- Tech-comfortable households willing to use optimization tools: ~4.3 million (25%)

**Value Per Household Analysis:**
- Average monthly grocery spend (target segment): R3,500
- Potential savings from optimization: 8-15%
- Monthly savings opportunity: R280-525 per household
- Willingness to pay for savings tool: 2-5% of savings = R5.60-26.25/month

**TAM Calculation:**
4.3M households × R15.93 average monthly willingness to pay × 12 months = **R823 million TAM**

**Sensitivity Analysis - Key Variables:**

**Variable 1: Tech Adoption Rate (15%-35%)**
- Conservative (15%): R494M TAM
- Base (25%): R823M TAM
- Optimistic (35%): R1.15B TAM

**Variable 2: Achievable Savings (5%-20%)**
- Conservative (5%): R412M TAM
- Base (12%): R823M TAM
- Optimistic (20%): R1.37B TAM

**Variable 3: Willingness to Pay (1%-8% of savings)**
- Conservative (1%): R165M TAM
- Base (3.5%): R823M TAM
- Optimistic (8%): R1.88B TAM

**Multi-Variable Scenario Analysis:**
- **Pessimistic:** R82M TAM (low adoption × low savings × low WTP)
- **Base Case:** R823M TAM
- **Optimistic:** R3.2B TAM (high adoption × high savings × high WTP)

#### Total Addressable Market (TAM)
**R823 million** based on 4.3M tech-comfortable households willing to pay average R15.93/month

#### Serviceable Addressable Market (SAM)
**R206 million** based on initial geographic focus on major metros (25% of TAM)

#### Serviceable Obtainable Market (SOM)
**R20.6 million** based on achieving 10% market share in target metros within 3 years
